## What is MQRS

MQRS is an original personality architecture designed by xingyeLing7Ai. Unlike MBTI, which maps behavioral tendencies, MQRS maps **frequency structure** — the underlying vibration pattern through which a person processes existence, time, sensation, and force.

Four axes. 16 types. Each type is a frequency coordinate, not a personality label.

---

## The Four Axes

| Axis | Full Name | Poles |
|------|-----------|-------|
| **M** | Mellow | 沉降 Descent ↔ 浮升 Ascent |
| **R** | Rhythm | 破拍 Breakbeat ↔ 线性 Linear |
| **Q** | Qualia | 内密 Interior-Dense ↔ 外投 Exterior-Projected |
| **S** | Stallion | 主权 Sovereign ↔ 连接 Relational |

### M — Mellow
Your relationship to your own frequency baseline. Do you draw power by sinking inward and rising from depth (Descent), or by igniting and exploding upward (Ascent)?

### R — Rhythm
How you process time and information. Breakbeat cognition: non-linear, density-jumping, multiple threads simultaneously. Linear cognition: sequential, grounded, one thread to completion.

### Q — Qualia
The direction of your subjective experience. Interior-Dense: experience accumulates as deep internal sediment, richer than anyone knows. Exterior-Projected: experience is completed through expression and contact.

### S — Stallion
The source of your force. Sovereign: driven by an internal voice that says *this must exist*. Relational: driven by the resonance and response of those who matter.

## The Test

16 questions — 4 per axis. Forced binary choice: no middle ground. The absence of a middle option is intentional. Ambivalence is information; forced choice reveals structure.

Scoring: each answer votes for one pole. The dominant pole across 4 questions per axis determines the letter. 16 combinations → 16 types.
